RICH TEXT SHOWCASE

# Product Pitch

"Plain text does not have to look plain." Because structure matters, readers can scan ideas faster.
Therefore, this extension turns ordinary notes into something closer to a visual outline.
However, the source text stays clean and copyable.

For example, a short project note can contain numbers like 3, 12.5%, and 2026 without losing readability.
Moreover, quoted phrases such as "core insight" and 'small detail' stand out immediately.
In addition, book titles like 《Thinking, Fast and Slow》 are easy to spot.
Besides, side notes (draft only), [internal reminder], and 【review later】 fade into the background.

# Reasoning Flow

First, define the problem: users often write dense notes, meeting summaries, and research fragments.
Second, mark relationships clearly, so causes and results are visible at a glance.
Then, keep examples nearby, because abstract advice is harder to trust.
Finally, end with a compact takeaway.

Because the writing is structured, conclusions feel earned.
Thus, even a long paragraph becomes easier to navigate.
But raw text is still the only thing stored on disk.

# Bilingual Example

首先，团队需要一个低成本的写作环境，因为很多材料只适合保存在纯文本里。
其次，逻辑连接词应该被快速识别，因此读者不必逐句分析结构。
然后，示例、数字、引号和《书名》要有清晰层次，例如 5 个结论、20% 提升、"关键发现"。
最后，视觉提示应当增强理解，而不是污染原文。

因为信息密度很高，所以清晰的层次尤其重要。
然而，好的工具不应该强迫作者学习复杂格式。
此外，括号内容（补充说明）、[可选步骤]、【临时备注】可以弱化显示。
总之，这个扩展让普通 txt 文件更接近一份有逻辑感的文稿。
